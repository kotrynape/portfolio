Kotryna Petraityte

Hello and welcome! I’m Kotryna (Koh-TREE-nah) Petraityte (don’t worry about it). As a translator by training and a lifelong lover of literature, I see writing in two main ways.

First, it’s the most trusted and powerful way to explain, convince, and expand knowledge in the most succinct and logical ways. This is where my fascination with technical writing comes in. Second, it’s an essential creative outlet - a way to dive into and, if we are lucky enough, expand the depths of ourselves. As you explore my portfolio, you’ll see both sides of my work.

Do you need to use language in one of these ways, or does your project combine both? Let’s talk!
