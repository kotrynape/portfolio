Social Media Communication
Short, engaging content goes a very — very — long way with today’s audience, but just as important is consistency in social media communication — no one trusts a brand that rarely posts. The optimal posting frequency varies by platform and industry, but for companies with high social media needs, I created 20 to 35 posts per month.
Google Ads and SEO
Google Ads are short, keyword-based ads that appear at the top of Google search results through paid promotion. SEO (search engine optimization) is the process of improving a website’s content and structure to help it rank higher in organic search results over time. It’s like buying Instagram followers versus building a genuine audience — the first gets you quick attention, but the second is more reliable and lasting. The answer is — both are necessary, as both of them help get you closer to the first page of Google search results, and with 93% of online experiences starting with a search engine, visibility is key.
