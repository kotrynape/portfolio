Social Media Communication

Short, engaging content goes a long way with today’s audience, but just as important is consistency — no one trusts a brand that rarely posts.

The optimal posting frequency varies by platform and industry, but for companies with high social media needs, I created 20 to 35 posts per month.
Google Ads and SEO
Google Ads and an SEO-optimized website: think of it as the difference between buying Instagram followers for quick attention and building a genuine, lasting audience.

In advertising, both strategies are essential for growing visibility, especially since 93% of online experiences begin with a search engine.

Creative Campaign Ideas
A well-planned campaign combines images, videos, and words — including catchy slogans and engaging, preferably CTA-driven copies. Is it worth it?

Absolutely — studies show that 64% of people are more likely to purchase after seeing branded content, and strong slogans can enhance brand recall by up to 80%.
